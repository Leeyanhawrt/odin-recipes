Pasta with Sausage Sauce

This is a dish that my girlfriend taught me how cook!

It's easy to make, fast, and the macronutrients are superb

  • Whole Grain Pasta
  • Extra Lean Ground Beef
  • Sausage Pasta Sauce
  1. Cook pasta in boiling water until aldante
  2. Cook ground beef with a bit of oil on skillet
  3. Combine beef and pasta into a wok and add pasta sauce, mix until everything is thoroughly coated
