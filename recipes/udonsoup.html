Udon Noodle Soup

Original Dish Owner

This is the first dish that I made by myself without the help of anyone else

The lightness of the soup and udon makes this the perfect dish for any occasion

  • Udon Noodles
  • Hotpot Beef
  • Chicken Stock
  • Vegetables
  1. Cook udon is boiling water combined with chicken stock
  2. Add vegetables into boiling water after 2 minutes have passed
  3. Add hotpot beef and cook for 1 minute before taking out to serve
